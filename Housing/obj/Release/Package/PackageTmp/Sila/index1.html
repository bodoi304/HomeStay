FAQ

Is YOKO HOUSE near
Da Lat city center?

It is located in Da Lat city center, far from airport 30km, near Thanh Buoi & Phuong Trang coach station if you travel from Sai Gon to Da Lat. The road leading to YOKO

What is check-in, check-out time?

Check-in time is from 12pm to late at night, check-out time is 11 am. The time can be earlier or later if your room is available before or after check-in/ check-out time. (E.g.: If there is no guest using your room after you check out, you can stay more without paying any fee. If there is a guest check-in right after you check out, please follow the check-out time as regulations.

What are advantages of YOKO?

It’s a special homestay located in city center operating as “cooking & culture” style:
staying here you can easily go to Da Lat market for buying food, cook by yourself.
free wifi.
free rice.
free coffee.
free jam strawberry.
free instant noodles for breakfast.
free a small kitchen with fully equipment
a front yard for parking (even for car).
2 bathrooms with water heaters
Toilet is the most concern at YOKO so that it is always kept clean.

Quite time?

If there are no planned special events, please kindly follow regulations on “Quite time” at YOKO HOUSE from 22:00pm to 6am of the next day, which ensures resting and relax time for other people.

What time do cherry blossoms bloom?

Normally, from the middle of January to the end of February, it depends on weather, the time shall be longer or shorter.

What time of the year do Mexican sunflower bloom?

Mexican sunflower beautifully blooms from November to the end of December.

How to rent a motorbike and how much?

To rent a motorbike, please book firstly with Ms. Huong: 0633.500.499, she will deliver the motorbike to YOKO. The price is from 80k to 120k/ 1 motorbike/ 1 day (you only need to provide ID card and pay the fee).
